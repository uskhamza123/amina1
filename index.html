About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! Graduated in fashion design and certified as a contemporary designer, I am passionate about the art of clothing creation. My work is strongly inspired by the Op Art style, an artistic movement that plays with optical illusions to create dynamic and captivating motifs. Over the years, I have perfected my design and illustration skills, and I master several design tools, especially for digital drawing and pattern creation. I invite you to explore my journey and my creations, and I remain at your disposal for any collaboration opportunities in the world of fashion and design.
